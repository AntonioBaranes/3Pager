Single People

Valentines Day is a time of year that always brings on reflection. Whether it be lost memories of a past lovers or the lack of a past lovers, Valentines Day can always bring something somber to the forefronts of our hearts and minds.

Boyfriends

Disclaimer: When I say boyfriends I don't mean all boyfriends. If you're reading this and you have a boyfriend, he loves Valentines Day so don't worry.

When Valentines Day comes around Boyfriends all over the world panic at the same time thinking about what they're going to do for Valentines Day. Flowers, chocolate, gifts, reservtion, etc... there's just so much. I'm not saying one's partner is not worth these things but it can be very stressfull. Also, this may not be for all relationships but in a lot of hetero-relationships the attention is given mainly to the girl. I think our society is progressive enough to the point that we could start seeing some girls take their guys out instead.
